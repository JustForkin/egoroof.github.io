Yandex Music Fisher 2014

Music downloader for Яндекс.Музыка and Яндекс.Радио (browser extension). It downloads single tracks, playlists, albums (with cover) and even whole artist discography; installs ID3 tag. You can resume interrupted downloads.

Browser ID3 Writer 2015

Pure JavaScript library for writing ID3 (v2.3) tag to MP3 files in browsers and Node.js. It was primarily developed for use by Yandex Music Fisher, but it's also used by Skyload and others.
